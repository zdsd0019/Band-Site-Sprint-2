Xavier de Rosnay & Gaspard Auge

Xavier de Ronsay and Gaspard Auge had a few mutual friends, who happened to run a record label, seeking tracks for a compilation. With an immediate connection, the pair decided to try thier hand at making song. That's when they created thier breakout single, We Are Your Friends - a feel-good, funk-filled, instant anthem that lost the remix contest, but won the attention of Pedro Winter, director of the newly founded Ed Banger Records, who boasted Daft Punk as former clients. One week later, they were in his office, signing a record deal.
